My Core Values

Safety-Driven Engineering

Reliability is non-negotiable in anesthesia. I design with fail-safe defaults, thorough testing, and clear boundaries between critical and non-critical paths.

Clinician-Centered Design

Tools should fit real operating-room workflows. I reduce cognitive load with simple, readable interfaces and decisions that are easy to trust.

Data-Informed, Privacy-Respecting

I use data and automation to assist judgment—never replace it—while keeping privacy and security built in from the start.
